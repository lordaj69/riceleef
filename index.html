Key Features

Instant Disease Detection

The AI tool analyzes images of rice leaves and detects diseases instantly. This saves time compared to traditional manual methods, enabling farmers to take quick action.

Cost-Effective Solution

By using AI-driven disease detection, farmers can reduce the need for expensive laboratory tests or field visits by experts, making the process more affordable.

User-Friendly and Accessible

Designed with simplicity in mind, the AI tool is easy to use for non-technical users. It can be accessed through a web interface, making it convenient for farmers in remote areas..

Sustainable Farming Support

Early and accurate disease detection helps reduce the overuse of pesticides, promoting eco-friendly farming practices and protecting the environment.
